Hotel Overview

The Omni Cancun Hotel & Villas is the perfect Mexico resort destination - whether you are considering a long weekend enjoying our luxurious accommodations or an extended vacation in a villa rental. The Omni Cancun Hotel is a five-star, Gran Tourism resort nestled between the dazzling Mexican Caribbean and secluded Nichupte Lagoon on Cancun Island. The Omni Cancun is a multi-year winner of the J.D. Power and Associates North American Hotel Guest Satisfaction Index Experience.

Enjoy our complete All-Inclusive package where you can enjoy unlimited meals and drinks in nine restaurants and five bars and experience total relaxation and fun without the hassle of carrying extra money around. The Omni Cancun Hotel and Villas Resort offers buffet and a la carte dining for breakfast, lunch and dinner with a choice of indoor, air-conditioned or outdoor terrace venues. Buffets offer a wide variety of choices suitable for the whole family. Gourmet Mexican, Italian and seafood a la carte dining venues are available for lunch and dinner. For the healthy minded, there's a new Sushi bar and all restaurants and room service include low calorie, low fat and low sodium Spa menus specially prepared by the chef. Outdoor bars offer snacks throughout the day along with your favorite beverages. Live music is presented nightly in the Lobby bar, and there is a weekly beach party with a traditional Mexican Fiesta. Twenty-four hour room service is available.

Our guest rooms and villas surround you with the spirit and tradition of the Mayan culture while offering modern hotel amenities. Expansive, pristine beaches, majestic lagoon sunsets, spectacular ocean views and unparalleled service make the Omni Cancun Hotel & Villas the perfect choice for your luxury resort getaway in breathtaking Cancun, Mexico.

INSPIRED BY MAYAN PAST AND MODERN COMFORT

The monumental entrance with its contemporary yet Mexican style leads Guests into the newly-renovated lobby featuring contemporary design with an exclusive check-in lounge, check-out and cashier`s desk, concierge desk, elegant and comfortable seating areas, and the inviting Lobby Bar. The color scheme uses neutrals combined with chocolate browns, Mayan stone statues and textures and materials inspired from the nature of the Yucatan Peninsula. The spectacular view from the lobby bar, the exquisite interior design, and the warmth of a genuine smile welcomes visitors to the Omni Cancun with gracious style. The heart of this tranquil resort is, without a doubt, its long tenured staff, many of whom have been at the hotel for more than a decade, and repeat Guests are quick to point out the level of service and attention to detail that goes above and beyond.

EXQUISITE GROUNDS WITH INTERCONNECTED POOLS

Lush gardens laced with coconut palm trees envelop the hotel, swathing the grounds in tropical sights and smells. Sprawling across the hotel grounds amid interesting Mayan relief sculptures are three large, interconnecting freshwater swimming pools. There are also cascading waterfalls, zero-grade entry, a swim-up bar, separate children's pool, and the incredible ocean view Mega Jacuzzi with seating for 40 guests. A Tropical Restaurant as well as the renowned Jacuzzi Beach Bar invite guests to a palm lined oceanfront area unmatched in Cancun.

ELEGANT UPSCALE ACCOMMODATIONS

The Omni Cancun Hotel's rooms, suites, and villas, characterized by their large size and beautiful marble floors, provide elegant, comfortable accommodations. Plus, all are non-smoking in compliance with local law. The well-appointed Guest quarters feature exclusively-designed contemporary furnishings, plush bedding, and superior amenities. Each room has a balcony with built-in, pillowed lounge- benches, MP3 clock radios, flat screen TVs with international channels, halogen reading lights, extra large electronic safes, hair-dryers, lighted makeup mirror, coffee machines, direct dial dual phones, iron and ironing boards, and more.

Restaurant

Savor the Flavors of Cancun Dining

The Omni Cancun Hotel & Villas gives you many wonderful restaurants and dining options to enjoy, all of your meals and beverages are now included with your stay.

Dine with a view, enjoy live music or cozy up for an intimate dinner by candle-light. Irresistible fare, tempting elixirs and wonderful atmosphere are the specials at every Omni Cancun Hotel & Villas restaurant or beachfront bar. Weekly theme nights include the Mexican Fiesta and Beach Party among others.

For the healthy minded, all of our restaurants and room service now include low calorie, low fat and low sodium Spa menus specially prepared by our chef.

Da Vinci Restaurant

Indulge in gourmet Italian cuisine in an intimate setting that surrounds you with replicas of the masters inventions and artwork. Excellent wine selection. Dinner only; reservations and proper attire required.. Open to Adults Only (18 years of age and older)

La Paloma

Alive with true Mexican warmth and flavor, La Paloma serves breakfast, lunch and dinner a la carte or buffet style. Wireless Internet access is available in La Paloma and its new open air terrace

Pina Colada

Fully-remodeled, this beachfront eatery and bar overlooks the turquoise Caribbean, and features seafood and grilled specialties for breakfast and lunch. Also available for special group parties and dinner.

Sushi Bar

The ocean view Sushi Bar serves a succulent selection of fresh sushi, rolls and sashimi as well as refreshing beverages to quench your thirst.

Kukus Jacuzzi Beach Bar

Located on the beach, this spectacular Jacuzzi Beach Bar for our guests 18 and older is where you can relax and have fun in an exotic atmosphere.

Lobby Bar

With a great Martini Menu this contemporary, Mexican bar offers live music in the evening, all in a cozy atmosphere.

Pool Grill and Bar

Serves light lunchtime specialties and features a full swim-up bar.
